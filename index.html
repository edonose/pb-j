The Perfect PB & J

Description

This Peanut Butter and jelly sandwich is my faveorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Any Peanut Butter and jam will work but my recommendation is to use grape jelly and all natural Peanut Butter

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of all natural Peanut Butter

Directions

  1. Get the first slice of bread
  2. Put jelly on that slice
  3. Get the other slice of bread
  4. put Peanut Butter on the other slice
  5. put slices together jelly and Peanut Butter inwards
